❓ Help & FAQ
Common questions about delivering with DirkMhob.
How do I accept an order? ▼
Go to the Orders tab. When you're online, new orders appear on the left. Click "Accept" to take an order — you'll see the route on the map.
How are delivery fees calculated? ▼
Each order has a fixed delivery fee set by the restaurant. You earn 100% of that fee. Track your totals on the Earnings page.
What happens if I reject an order? ▼
Rejected orders disappear from your list and become available to other drivers. There's no penalty — just stay online to keep receiving orders.
How do I mark an order as delivered? ▼
In the Orders tab under Active Deliveries, click "Mark as Delivered ✓" once you've handed over the food to the customer.
Who do I contact for support? ▼
Email driver-support@dirkmhob.com or reach us at +855 23 123 456 (Mon–Fri 8am–6pm). For urgent delivery issues, call the restaurant directly.
